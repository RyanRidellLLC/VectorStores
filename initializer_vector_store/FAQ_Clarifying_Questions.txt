*Sample Clarifying Questions (for Initializer Agent reference)*

1. What is your exact service area (cities or neighborhoods)?
2. What is the link to your Google Business Profile (GBP_URL)?
3. What are your top 5 proofs of credibility (media, awards, case studies)?
4. Are there any name collisions or other people with the same name we should differentiate from?
5. What is your primary offer and price point?
6. Which photos or videos are approved for homepage hero usage?
7. Are address and phone number public or private?
8. Which social media profiles should be considered official?
9. Do you have a mentions sheet or portfolio folder we can access?
10. What is your brand color palette or primary hex values?
11. Which logo and favicon should be used as defaults?
